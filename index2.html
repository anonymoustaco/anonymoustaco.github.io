Types:

To help give an idea of the different advanatages, each pulley will be assigned a bike gear from 1-3.


Fixed

Image Credit: slideplayer.com

These pulleys work by having a rope over a wheel. They are the least powerful of all the types. If it where to be on a bike, it would be on gear 3.


Moveable

Image Credit: etc.usf.edu

These have two wheels instead of one. This is the second powerful. If we where to be on a bike, this pulley would have us cruising on gear 2.


Compound

How do they work?


They use mechanical advantage to reduce the amout of force needed. But like everything, there is a flipside. it requires you to pull for longer than you would normaly without a pulley.
