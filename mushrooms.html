Rosemary butter mushrooms

    You will need...
  • 1/2 container Mushrooms, stems out, halfed. (quartered if they are HUGE)
  • 1 Tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • sprig of rosemary
  • a large skillet (idealy cast iron, if you have it)

The Recipe:

  1. Bring the skillet up to medieum-high, lubricated with olive oil.
  2. When up to heat, put in the mushrooms.
  3. once in the pan, move them swifly around in the pan.
    (you may need to add oil as the mushrooms soak it up)
  4. cook until they begin to realse fliud, then kill the heat,
    add the remaing ingredents, cook for 3 more minutes,
    and stir rapidly.
  5. allow to cool
  6. enjoy!!

**TIP** These are a good side for steak.
